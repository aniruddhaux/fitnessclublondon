How is the fitness club different from other gyms?

We know that if a gym makes you feel uncomfortable, or fails to meet your needs you won't stay a member for long. Here at The Fitness Club, we pride ourselves in not being your traditional gym. You won't find more bodies than machines, or grunting men lifting weights to intimidate you. Instead, you'll experience a small and exclusive club with limited membership. This allows you the space and confidence to get fit along with making your time with us enjoyable.

Friendly and family orientated in our approach – you'll find all ages, shapes and sizes here, that's not to say we don't take fitness seriously. We do! We present a new concept in gym work, concentrating on looking after the members we've got, rather than super sizing our numbers.

No expense is spared on equipment. We have installed 40 stations of gold standard machines – we use Technogym, the official choice for the Athletes' Village for the 2012 Olympics. All cardio machines have their own personal LCD TV screens, so just plug in your headphones and you're ready to burn those calories!

For muscle tone, we have new generation resistance machines together with a free weights section. For an end of session cool-down, make your way to our stretching area. And to ensure your visits are hassle free, we also provide free lockers and complimentary towels for both your workout and showering.

The Fitness Club is a place to feel relaxed about your body – there's no awkward atmosphere, only a supportive one, where we strive to be brilliant both in terms of training, and the advice we give on helping you reach your goals.

We're a new type of club for members who are looking for something special.
