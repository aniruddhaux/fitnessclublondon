Umana Noordeen [ Msc MCSP ]

  • - Musculoskeletal and Sports Physiotherapist
  • - Acupuncture Practitioner
  • - Injury prevention and corrective exercise specialist

Umana Noordeen qualified as a physiotherapist in 2004 from the University of Birmingham with honours and went onto complete her Masters in Advanced Physiotherapy in 2009. She is a highly experienced musculoskeletal physiotherapist with over 10 years of practice working with various institutions in the Maldives and the UK.

In addition to private practice Umana's interest in sports physiotherapy has lead her to work with athletes ranging from track and field Olympic medallists to amateur marathon runners. Umana is the lead physiotherapist for the Maldives Olympic committee and looked after her home team at the London 2012 Olympics and Glasgow 2014 Common Wealth Games. She has also gained valuable experience working at elite training camps in Jamaica. Umana believes in health promotion and focuses her practice on addressing the root causes of injury through biomechanical analysis and injury prevention methods.

Umana continues to promote healthy living and keeps herself informed with current research and innovative practices to ensure her patients get the best possible treatments. She believes in a multi disciplinary team approach to patient care and works closely with consultants and personal trainer Dean Schaffer

All clients requiring Physio are seen at our Harley Street Medical Center.
